About the Practice

A coordination practice focused on European vehicle certification

EntryMotion was founded to address a specific gap facing international OEMs: the distance between engineering a vehicle and certifying it for European roads. We are not a large consultancy or a certification body — we are a specialized coordination practice, grounded in the operational reality of EU homologation and type approval.

Our work sits between regulatory strategy and its implementation. We coordinate the people, documentation, testing schedules, and approval processes that move a vehicle programme from technical readiness through the regulatory milestones — and we stay with the programme until those milestones are met.

From first-time market entrants to established manufacturers expanding their European footprint, we act as the local coordination interface between your engineering organization and Europe's regulatory framework.
